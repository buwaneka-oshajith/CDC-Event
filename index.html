Love Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka’s Independence day celebrations for the public. A festival of Sri Lankan food, culture, Art & Crafts, Music, Dance and Drama.

Sanchraka Udawa

The Sri Lanka Association Of Inbound Tour Operators (SLAITO) Together With Sri Lanka Tourism Will Organize The 9th EDITION Of Sancharaka Udawa “Tourism Product Trade Fair” On 07th and 08th of June 2019, At The Sirimavo Bandaranaike Memorial Exhibition Center (BMICH). This Exhibition Of Tourism Product And Services Will Create A Platform For All The Tourism Industry Stake Holders To Meet And Network To Enhance The Awareness Of Their Product And Services.

Culinary Arts Food Expo

Culinary Art Food Expo the most comprehensive Food, Beverage & Hospitality show in Sri Lanka is back again for the 19th consecutive time. The Culinary Art Food Expo 2019 will cover food products and services from processing to consuming stage covering culinary and hospitality segments, in short every aspect of food "From the Factory to the Table" will be covered at the EXPO.


Over 2000 Chefs from Sri Lanka will compete for Culinary Excellence during the three days of Culinary Art Food Expo which is bought to you by the Chefs Guild of Lanka. Culinary Art Food Expo is the only event in this part of the world where over 2000 culinary professionals meet under one roof.


Culinary Art Food Expo attracts exhibitors from all over the world while buyers representing various segments visit Culinary Art Food Expo to view and obtain the newest products and services available and to view the latest technology on offer.

Agriculture Expo

Agriculture Expo 2019 The National Agriculture Exhibition and Farmer's Convention will be a showcase where the latest technology, equipment, machinery and ideas are on display.


The objective is to ensure that the farmers of Sri Lanka use Agro-Technology to sustain the world's growing population while working efficiently with the latest farming advances,producing quality Sri Lankan produce for the local and international market.

FHAM

FHAM Exhibition and International Culinary the leading annual festive for the Hospitality Industry of Maldives gathers the entire Tourism Industry to meet under-one roof during the event. It attracts the Owners, Senior Management and other staff of Resorts, Hotels, Guest Houses, Safari Boat Operators and Owners, Restaurants and Cafeterias to visit the Exhibition that showcases a wide range of products and services offered by International and Local companies to the Tourism Industry of Maldives


With more than 110 stalls represented by more than 50 companies from Local, Regional and International demonstrating their products and services to the Hospitality Industry of Maldives makes it the largest gathering of the Industry in the Maldives. Food & Beverage, Kitchen Equipments and Systems, Amenities, Cutleries and Crockery, Furniture and many more product ranges that required by the Hospitality Industry are been presented and offered.

Hotel Show Colombo

South Asia's most successful hospitality trade exhibition, Hotel Show Colombo 2019 will be held on 26th – 28th July 2018 at the BMICH, Colombo. Hotel Show Colombo 2019 is being organized by the Ceylon Hotel School Graduates Association together with CDC Events and Travels (Pvt) Ltd along with the support of the state and private sector.


Hotel Show Colombo, which brings together many hospitality brands is recognized to be the only exhibition of this nature taking place in Sri Lanka and has a strong support of government and tourism authorizes. Hotel Show Colombo 2018 will be promoted worldwide and a larger number of foreign exhibitors and high end buyers are expected to participate at this event.

Asia Logistic Forum

CDC Events & Travels together with Gensoft (Pvt) Ltd has organized the ‘Asia Logistics Forum 2018’ which will be taking place on the 4th& 5th of September 2018 at Cinnamon Grand, Colombo, Sri Lanka. This forum is expected to attract a minimum of 200 delegates from the South Asian region.


The significance of having this Forum is to bring about the Logistic industry and all related logistic corporations in the South Asian region together for a discussion on how they can further improvethe Logistics Industry.At this forum, new ideas and concepts on improving the industry and how it can help contribute more in assisting the development of the Economy will also be discussed.


This Forum is designed to create a two-way information flow that provides individual meetings, specialtechnical and networkingsessions in a professional Conference environment. We believe that both local and international delegates who seek better understanding of the importance of logistics &supply chaincan have an open discussion with each other. This forum will help gain knowledge as how to develop a strategy to promote their logistical services and likewise understand the critical nature of moving products faster and more cost effectively.

Sri Lanka Buildcon

The exhibition was first conceptualized and organized in Sri Lanka in 2012 to bring all the architects, designers, developers, engineers, contracting companies, material suppliers and technology providers under one roof for displaying and sourcing products and the journey continues year after year. 8th Sri Lanka Buildcon International Expo 2019 is solely dedicated to the fast growing and the very popular construction industry. The construction industry is amongst one of the fastest growing sectors in Sri Lanka and comes with a promising future. As the national economy grows the social and financial demand for infrastructural update also increases.

Sri Lanka Wood Expo

Wood Industry of Sri Lanka has evolved to greater heights and Sri Lanka Wood Expo 2019 will be the industry's key Exhibition, it creates an opportunity for investments, joint ventures, technology and innovation transfers through one platform of worldwide companies of the industry. Sri Lanka Wood Expo plays a prominent role because it has so much to offer. This is confirmed by the extremely positive response elicited from the display of wood materials, wood services and Wood equipment. Sri Lanka Wood Expo is an international exhibition, which showcases products from across the globe. SRI LANKA WOOD EXPO 2018 will deliver unprecedented access to key decision-makers in wood and related industries. The Expo will greatly increase the future business prospects. Wood & Wood Based Industry of Sri Lanka is performing as one of the important manufacturing sector with a wide array of products. The Wood Based Industry has been spread all.


The Sri Lanka Wood Expo 2019 in this aspect turned out to be great success for the exhibitors as well as the visitors. Last year's expo was supported by Ceylon Institute of Builders, Wood Based Industrial Association and many more. The industry benefited with the wide spectrum of networking that the expo offered. Similarly we expect the same from 2019.

Lankapak

The premier international packaging exhibition in Sri Lanka, LANKAPAK 2024 will be held on the 11th, 12th, 13th September 2019 at the BMICH in Colombo for the 21th spectacular time. This event was initiated by the Sri Lanka Institute of Packaging in the year 1981 with the objective of facilitating the printing and packaging industry to showcase their valued products and services to various other industries that require their services on a large scale.


Lankapak 2024 is organized by the Sri Lanka Institute of Packaging together with CDC Events along with the support of government and private sector organizations. The Exhibition will comprise over 150 stalls displaying thousands of brands to meet all kinds of packaging requirements under one roof.

Sri Lanka Epidemiological Association

IEA promotes using the highest quality of epidemiological practice to solve public health problems throughout the world. We do this through scientific meetings, seminars and training courses; the publication of journals and other materials; through networking and the exchange of ideas among members; and through other activities consistent with these aims and aligned with our principles of universality, decentralization and volunteerism.

Boat Show

The boat show & Fisheries Exhibition Sri Lanka is the one and only boat exhibition and trade fair in Sri Lanka. The Fifth Boat Show & Fisheries Exhibition will be held on 13th to 15th October of 2016 at Dickowita Fisheries Harbour which is expected to attract local and foreign buyers and boating enthusiast from the leisure, marine, tourism, fishing and all other related industries.


The valued support extended by the Ministry of Industry and Commerce, Sri Lanka Export development Board, Boat Building Technology Improvement Institute, Sri Lanka Navy, NIKINI Automation systems, Neil Marine, was a key factor to make the Boat Show Sri Lanka 2012 a success.
